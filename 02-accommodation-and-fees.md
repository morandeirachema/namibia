# Accommodation & park fees — verified against the official 2026/2027 rate card

Source: **NWR Rack Rates 2026/2027, "01 November 2026 – 31 October 2027"**
https://www.nwr.com.na/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/NWR-Rack-Rates-2026-2027.pdf
(downloaded and read directly, 2026-07-16)

> ## ⚠️ Read this first: the rate card everyone quotes online does not cover your trip
>
> NWR prices on a **November–October rate year**, not a calendar year. The card circulating
> on every travel blog and reseller site is **"01 Nov 2025 – 31 Oct 2026"** — it **expires
> before you land** if you travel in November or December.
>
> The correct card for your window is **2026/2027**, and within it your dates fall in the
> **cheaper "November 2026 – June 2027" column**.
>
> **October 2026 is priced on the OLD card, in its dearest "July–October 2026" tier.**
> So NWR has a season boundary on **1 November** — the same week as the rental price cliff.

## 💰 The October → November drop is larger for accommodation than for the car

Same unit, same camp, one week apart:

| Unit | October 2026 (old card, high tier) | November 2026 (new card, low tier) | Saving/night |
|---|---|---|---|
| Okaukuejo camping (2 pax) | N$1,120 | **N$920** | N$200 (~€10) |
| Okaukuejo **Waterhole chalet** (2 pax) | N$6,960 | **N$4,760** | **N$2,200 (~€110)** |
| Okaukuejo Double room (2 pax) | N$4,760 | **N$3,800** | N$960 (~€48) |

Over a 14-day lodge-based trip this compounds into **many hundreds of euros** — on top of the
**~€870** saved on the 4x4. The commercial case for travelling **after 15 November is now very
strong**. Whether the weather and game viewing destroy that advantage is the open question.

That is NWR itself signalling that late-season conditions change how the parks operate.

## Park fees (from pass 1 — see `01-verified-findings.md`)

Rose **80–100% on 1 April 2026**: ~**N$280 per international adult per day** at Etosha,
Namib-Naukluft/Sossusvlei and Ai-Ais/Fish River Canyon, **plus ~N$60 vehicle**, charged
**per park, per 24 hours from entry**.

**Budget ~N$620/day for two adults + car**, at each park, for each 24-hour unit.

The N$150 figure on most travel sites is the obsolete 2021 schedule (refuted 0–3).
